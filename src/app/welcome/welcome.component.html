This is a proof of concept of a webshop in angular

You can't actually buy anything, nor are there any real products here. This is just an experiment for me to learn and demonstrate angular. You'll find the code here.

How to use this?

  • Assume you've sucessfully logged into your hypothetical account.
  • You then want to browse some products starting from the categories listed on the left.
  • Once you've found a product that you would want to buy (hypothetically), you add it to the cart or to your watchlist.
  • You can look at the current state of the cart or your watchlist by clicking on the respecive menu items in the top right.
  • Once you're happy with whatever you have in the cart, you can make a hypothetical purchase, by viewing your cart (top right), and then click on "Hypothetical Purchase".
  • You can view your previous hypothetical purchases, by clicking on "Hypothetical purchase history" in the top right menu.
  • Hint: Everything is happening in your browser, so nothing is stored permantly like purchases or carts or watchlists. So as soon as your session is over, or your browser clears cache or what not, everything gets deleted.
